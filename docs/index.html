Educational System

A two-section AI-assisted learning platform. Tap a card to explore.

👩‍🏫 Teachers

Pick a subject and upload its outcome (PDF) & past papers. The AI generates aligned questions.

🧑‍🎓 Students

Open a subject to get practice, detailed feedback, and — for languages — a personal dictionary, shadowing & استشهادات.

👩‍🏫 Teacher — choose a subject

Tap a subject to upload its outcome (PDF) and past papers.

🧑‍🎓 My subjects

Choose your level, then a subject.
